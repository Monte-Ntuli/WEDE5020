Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Jane Doe, Monte Solutions began as a boutique event planner in Johannesburg, South Africa. Over the past decade, we've:


  • Organized 500+ events across 3 continents
  • Pioneered hybrid event technology during the 2020 pandemic
  • Won the 2024 African Events Excellence Award
  • Developed proprietary event management software (MontePro™)
